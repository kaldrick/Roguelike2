Star Bloom

Star Bloom is intended to highlight areas of your scene which fall within a certain threshold value.  Star Bloom can be used to give a bloom effect or a star glare effect depending on your Radius and Offset settings.

Offset:  Float
This setting refers to the number of pixels to skip between iterations.  It can be used to fake a larger radius for the effect.  Associated variable:  offset_str

Threshold Min:  Float
Areas below this luminance threshold will not be highlighted.  Associated variable:  threshold_min_str

Threshold Max:  Float
Areas above this luminance threshold will not be highlighted.  Associated variable:  threshold_max_str

Amount:  Float
How much of the effect is added to the rendered scene.  Associated variable:  amount_str

Radius:  Float
How large the stars will appear.  A radius of 7 plus an offset of 2 will give a visible radius of 14.  Associated variable:  radius_str

Enable or disable Star Bloom by modifying the StarBloomEnabled boolean variable.
